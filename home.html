Our Library Management System (LMS) provides to Students a good

platform to discover books in our Library.

A student is able to see whether the book he/she wants is available

in the library or not.

Also, they can confirm their withdrawal of any book they want on

this website.

This means once they confirm their withdrawal of book, then that

book will be not issued by other.

This marks that book as unavailable.

Also, that book is marked unavailable for only 3 days.

After 3 days, confirmation of withdrawal is cancelled.

Student can register here on this website to get updates of our library.

Student can login to their account as well, to make advantage of buying

any book.

For any query or suggestion, student can submit it on contact page of our website.
